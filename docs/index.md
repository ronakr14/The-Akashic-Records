## 🧠 Purpose

To **capture**, **organize**, **distill**, and **express** information across all domains of my life — from data engineering to mystic philosophy, from career goals to daily insights.

This vault is my **second brain** — designed for clarity, velocity, and depth of thinking.

---

## 🔮 Structure

Based on the **PARA** method:

* 📁 **Projects** — Active, goal-driven tasks with a clear outcome.
* 🗂️ **Areas** — Ongoing responsibilities & focus zones.
* 📚 **Resources** — Notes, references, and raw materials.
* 🗃️ **Archive** — Dormant or completed content.

- Additional spaces:

* 🌀 `Inbox/` – Fleeting ideas and quick captures
* 🧬 `Templates/` – Reusable structures for consistency

---

## ⚙️ Powered By

* **Dendron**: Hierarchical, VSCode-native PKM built for scale and developer productivity.
* **VSCode Extensions**: Customizable tooling ecosystem to optimize workflows.
* **Dataview-like Queries**: Native hierarchical note queries for data-driven insight retrieval.
* **Graph Views**: Visualize knowledge relationships at scale.
* **Integrated CLI and API**: Automate, customize, and extend your PKM environment.

---

## 🧩 Methodology

Built on the **CODE** process:

1. ✍️ **Capture** – Everything flows into `Inbox` or `Daily Notes`.
2. 🗂 **Organize** – Notes are filed into the PARA hierarchy.
3. 💡 **Distill** – High-signal content is refined & summarized.
4. 🗣 **Express** – Ideas become outputs: guides, apps, talks, etc.
