## The 80/20 Learning Path

1. **Variables & Data Types** – Core types (str, int, float, list, dict, tuple) are 80% of real usage.
2. **Control Flow** – If/else, for/while loops, and comprehensions streamline most automation logic.
3. **Functions & Scopes** – Understanding `def`, `*args`, `**kwargs`, closures, and lambda makes code reusable.
4. **Modules & Packages** – Learn `import`, `__name__`, and how to structure scripts into reusable chunks.
5. **OOP in Python** – Grasp `class`, `__init__`, `self`, and inheritance for scalable codebases.
6. **Exceptions & Error Handling** – `try/except/finally` is essential for robust programs.
7. **File I/O & OS Interaction** – Scripts often read/write files and interact with system paths.
8. **Virtual Environments & pip** – Isolate environments and manage dependencies cleanly.

(Extras: List/dict comprehensions, f-strings, decorators, context managers)
